    Experience

    Fugro

    Over the summer, I worked in a professional software team for the oil company Fugro to help produce a control panel user interface. The UI is intended for specialists operating offshore ROVs and was built on the Qt framework.

    I was tasked with creating QML components from Figma designs and coded primarily in QML, but also created custom components with C++.

    2020

    Attendr App

    Following the hackathon, I was invited by the creators of Attendr to produce an interactive demo for their app.

    The demo consists of replicas of the student and lecturer versions of Attendr and are fully interactive. It also includes tooltips to walk the user through the app.

    The owners were pleased with the demo and included it on the app's official website.
